About Me

Hi, my name is Noah Herron. I have loved computers since the young age of 10 when my parents purchased our first dell computer. I found myself fascinated with the internet and a game called "Runescape". After countless hours on the internet I began to realize I wanted a career involving computers.

I currently work at a Taco Bell on 24 Hwy as a assistant manager. Taco Bell has been my only job that I started at the age of 18. I decided to change my career path after 3 years when I discovered Code Academy. After a couple of courses I realized coding was for me, this is how I found myself at Kansas's Coding Bootcamp.
